## Some notes: 

1. buidling_id for town hall is 1.
2. building_Id for huts = 2...num_huts. 
3. building_Id for walls starts from num_huts + 2. 
3. Cannon always attacks the closest troop.
4. 'r' triggers rage spell
5. 'h' triggers heal spell
6. '1' = spawn at 1st spawn pt
7. '2' = spawn at 2nd spawn pt
8. '3' = spawn at spawn 3rd pt
